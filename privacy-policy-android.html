Privacy Policy – Appointments & Reports (Android)

This Privacy Policy explains how Appointments & Reports handles your data in the Android version of the app.

Developer

This app is developed by Appointments & Reports.

Data we collect

  • Clients: name, phone number, email, birthday and appointment details.
  • Appointments: date, time, service, notes, price.
  • Financial data: income and expenses you enter manually.
  • Other configuration: reminders and time-off information that you configure.

Contacts permission (READ_CONTACTS)

  • On Android, the app can read your contacts only when you explicitly choose to import a client from the phone’s Contacts.
  • We do NOT upload your entire contacts list anywhere.
  • We only use the selected contact’s name, phone number and email to create a client in the app.

Where data is stored

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • If you sign in with Google, your data can also be synchronized with your personal Firebase account (Cloud Firestore) in the path users/{your UID}. This is used only to keep your data in sync across your own devices.

What we do NOT do

  • We do NOT sell your data.
  • We do NOT show ads.
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties, except the infrastructure providers needed to run the app (Google / Firebase).

Your choices

  • You can edit or delete clients, appointments, income, expenses and reminders at any time inside the app.
  • You can delete all local data from the app settings.
  • If you use cloud sync, you can also delete your Firebase data from within the app or directly from your Firebase account.
  • You can also request full deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, you can contact us at: c_mihail@icloud.com.
